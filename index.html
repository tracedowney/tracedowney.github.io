A portfolio that lets the work stay connected.

Some projects start on a screen. Others start with fabric, wood, paper, tools, or a camera. The point of this site is to let those threads stay connected without flattening them into one generic vibe.

It should feel clear on the first pass, but still have enough personality and range that the whole body of work makes sense together.

Different materials, shared instincts.

The through-line is problem-solving with taste, patience, and curiosity. Each area gets its own texture, but the whole site still feels like one person made it.

Why it works

Big enough for all the side things. Focused enough to still feel like me.

I do not want this site to read like a random list of hobbies. I want it to make the case that the same strengths show up whether I am building an interface, planning a quilt, setting a stone, documenting a renovation, or shaping a piece of wood.

That means clarity, care, and a little personality in every section. It also means giving each category enough room to breathe instead of forcing everything into one narrow template.
