Why write at all

The thinking is part of the portfolio.

A lot of value gets lost when a project is shown only as a finished artifact. Writing gives context, records the reasoning, and makes the work more useful to other people.

It also helps me keep track of what I am actually learning, which is often the most reusable part of the whole process.
